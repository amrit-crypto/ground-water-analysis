Contamination Sources

Hidden Threats
Underground

Groundwater contamination originates from diverse anthropogenic and geological sources. Early detection is critical to preventing irreversible aquifer degradation.

Step 01
Source Identification
Mapping all potential contaminant sources within the watershed — industrial facilities, agricultural zones, and septic systems.
Step 02
Geological Assessment
Analyzing rock formations, soil permeability, and fault lines to model contaminant transport pathways through the subsurface.
Step 03
Sampling & Lab Analysis
Collecting stratified water samples at multiple depths, analyzed for 120+ chemical and microbiological parameters.
Step 04
Risk Modeling & Reporting
Generating predictive contamination plume models and issuing remediation recommendations with priority rankings.
🏭
Industrial Effluents
Heavy metals (chromium, lead, cadmium) and volatile organic compounds from manufacturing discharge seep through unsaturated zones into shallow aquifers.
🌾
Agricultural Runoff
Nitrates, phosphates, and pesticide residues from fertilizer overuse create a diffuse pollution source that's extremely difficult to remediate once established.
🪨
Geogenic Arsenic
Naturally occurring arsenic mobilized by oxidizing pyrite minerals in sedimentary rocks. Prevalent in alluvial plains and deltaic regions.
🌊
Saltwater Intrusion
Overpumping of coastal aquifers allows seawater to migrate inland, causing permanent salinization of freshwater lenses in island and coastal systems.
🏘️
Urban Leachate
Landfill leachate, fuel spills, and aging sewage infrastructure create localized contamination plumes in peri-urban groundwater systems.
Remediation Technologies

Restoring Purity

Once contamination is characterised, we deploy targeted treatment technologies matched to the specific pollutant chemistry and hydrogeological conditions.

🔵
Reverse Osmosis
Semi-permeable membranes remove dissolved salts, heavy metals, nitrates, and organic compounds. Achieves 95–99% rejection rates for most contaminants.
☀️
UV Disinfection
Germicidal ultraviolet light inactivates pathogens including bacteria, viruses, and protozoa without introducing chemical residuals.
🌿
Phytoremediation
Hyperaccumulator plants such as sunflower and Indian mustard extract heavy metals from shallow aquifer zones through their root systems.
⚗️
Activated Carbon
Granular activated carbon adsorption removes pesticides, pharmaceuticals, chlorinated solvents, and taste/odour compounds with high efficiency.
🔬
Bioremediation
Engineered microbial communities degrade petroleum hydrocarbons, chlorinated solvents, and organic contaminants in situ without excavation.
💎
Ion Exchange
Resin beads selectively replace harmful ions (nitrate, arsenic, fluoride) with benign ions, producing high-quality treated water at scale.
